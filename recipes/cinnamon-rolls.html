Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • ⅓ cup butter
  • 1 (.25 ounce) package active dry yeast
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 4 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 eggs

Filling

  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • ½ cup raisins

Steps

  1. Cook for 10 minutes
  2. Use plates, salt and oil
  3. Put everything in refrigerator
  4. Eat it
